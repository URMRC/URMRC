URMRC : Upper Rhine Mobile Robotic Challenge

The URMRC (Upper Rhine Mobile Robotic Challenge) is a robotics competition developed, organized, and conducted within the framework of the RobotHub Academy initiative. It takes place during the BE 5.0 Industries of the Future trade fair and conference, which attracts around 4,000 visitors.

The first edition of the competition was held in 2024, followed by the second edition in 2025. Each edition of this cross-border competition brought together eight teams, representing a total of 24 Bachelor's and Master's students from several higher education institutions across the Upper Rhine region in France and Germany.
